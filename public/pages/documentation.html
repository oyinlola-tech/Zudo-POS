Getting Started Guide

Welcome to Zudo POS. This guide will help you set up your workspace, configure your first inventory items, and process your inaugural transaction in under 10 minutes.

1

Create Your Workspace

Login to your dashboard and navigate to the Settings > General section. Here you'll define your shop name, currency (₦ by default), and tax rates.

2

Onboard Hardware

Connect your thermal printer and barcode scanner via USB or Bluetooth. The POS will automatically detect most devices. For specific Nigerian market printers, please refer to our Hardware Setup list.

bluetooth

Wireless Sync

Best for mobile tablet registers.

usb

Direct USB

Highest reliability for busy counters.

3

Import Inventory

Upload a CSV file containing your product list or add items manually using our tactile "Quick-Add" interface. Remember to set your **Alert Thresholds** for low-stock notifications.

Pro Tip: Zebra Lists

Our UI uses "Zebra Striping" instead of borders to help cashiers scan item rows faster during peak traffic. Look for the subtle tonal shifts in the product grid.
