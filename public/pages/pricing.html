Nigerian Business FAQs

Everything you need to know about operating with The Zudo.

Does it work when the internet is down?

Yes. Our "Fluid Sync" technology allows you to keep selling even during network outages. Transactions are stored locally and automatically sync to the cloud once connectivity is restored.

How does it handle Bank Transfers?

We integrate with major Nigerian banks and payment switches. For the Business plan, the system generates a unique dynamic account number for each transaction, automatically confirming payment within seconds.

Do I have to buy hardware from you?

No. We are hardware-agnostic. Zudo POS runs on Android, iPadOS, and Web. If you already have thermal printers or barcode scanners, we can likely integrate them via Bluetooth or USB.

Can I manage multiple stores from one phone?

Absolutely. The owner's dashboard provides a consolidated view of all your locations. You can track stock movements between your Lekki and Ikeja branches in real-time from anywhere in the world.
