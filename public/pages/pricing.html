Common Questions

Expert solutions for the unique Nigerian retail landscape.

What happens when the internet goes out?

Zudo POS is built for connectivity challenges. Our "Offline Guard" ensures you can keep selling even without a connection. Your data will sync automatically as soon as the network returns.

Do you support POS hardware already in my shop?

Yes, Zudo is hardware-agnostic. We work with most Bluetooth and USB receipt printers, barcode scanners, and drawer systems common in the Nigerian market.

Is there a limit on transactions?

None at all. All plans include unlimited transactions. We believe your growth shouldn't be penalized by higher fees.
