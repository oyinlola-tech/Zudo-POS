At a Glance

We've designed our POS to be the "Fluid Authority" in retail. This means we prioritize your transaction speed and data integrity above all else. This document outlines our Mutual Responsibility Framework.

Data Protection & Privacy

Your retail data—including inventory levels, customer profiles, and transaction history—is treated with the highest level of encryption. We utilize Tonal Architecture in our security protocols to ensure that sensitive financial information is siloed from general application logs.

  • End-to-end encryption for all ₦ currency transaction data.
  • Bi-weekly automated security audits and penetration testing.
  • Zero-knowledge architecture for merchant payment credentials.
  • Compliance with local Nigerian financial data regulations.

User Responsibilities

To maintain the integrity of "Zudo POS" workspace, users must adhere to specific operational standards. High-speed retail environments require precise input and secure access management.

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of cashier and admin login credentials.
  • Ensuring hardware compatibility with our minimalist barcode scanning interface.
  • Prompt reporting of any unauthorized transaction patterns.
  • Adherence to the "No-Line" rule in physical workspace layouts to match UI ergonomics.
