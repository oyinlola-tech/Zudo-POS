Frequently Asked Questions

Quick solutions for common operational queries.

arrow_forward How do I integrate a new barcode scanner?

Our POS is plug-and-play compatible with 95% of standard USB scanners. Simply connect and go to Settings > Hardware to calibrate.

arrow_forward Does it work without an internet connection?

Yes. Our Fluid Sync technology stores transactions locally and automatically pushes them to the cloud once service is restored.

arrow_forward Can I manage multiple branch inventories?

The Administrative Hub allows real-time monitoring and stock transfers between unlimited physical locations from a single dashboard.

arrow_forward Is the hardware warranty included?

All Enterprise tier hardware comes with a 24-month replacement guarantee. Support cases are handled within 6 hours in major metropolitan hubs.
